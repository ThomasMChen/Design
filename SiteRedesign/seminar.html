Wabash Modern Analysis Seminar

Wabash College Seminars • IUPUI Wabash Conferences

The Wabash Extramural Modern Analysis Seminar meets four or five times each year on Saturday afternoon at Wabash College, Crawfordsville, Indiana for conversation and two talks on analysis. Also, each fall, the Seminar holds a weekend miniconference in the area. The Seminar is organized by Hari Bercovici and David Fisher, Indiana University; Zhong-Jin Ruan, University of Illinois; Marius Dadarlat, Purdue University; and Ron Ji, IUPUI; and is hosted by Esteban Poffald, Wabash College.
